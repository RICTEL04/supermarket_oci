Quick Fixes

Method 1: Direct Settings

Open Chrome settings directly:

chrome://settings/content/microphone

Make sure "Sites can ask to use your microphone" is enabled.

Method 2: Site Settings

View all site permissions:

chrome://settings/content/all

Find your site (localhost:3000) and reset permissions if needed.
